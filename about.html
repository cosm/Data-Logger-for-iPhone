Data Logger enables you to store and graph any data of your choosing along with a timestamp and geolocation. The application uses Pachube.com, a realtime data brokerage for sensor, energy and environment data from objects, devices and buildings around the world. Pachube provides a suite of applications for visualizing, mapping and making sense of the data you have stored.

You might use Data Logger to store electricity meter readings, to create maps of pollution or temperature sensor readings around your neighbourhood, or animal sightings around the city.

You can also set up custom data feeds, with user-defined min and max values, tags, description and units.

Your data can then be used in a suite of visualizing, conversion and mapping applications available at: http://apps.pachube.com/.

To use the application you will need a Pachube account. Sign up here: http://www.pachube.com/signup.
